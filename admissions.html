General Requirements

Requirements may vary depending on the level or program.

  • Completed enrollment or inquiry form
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Recent 1x1 or 2x2 photo, if required
  • Previous school records, if applicable
  • Other documents requested by the school office

Enrollment Steps

  1. Contact the school office.
  2. Submit the required information or documents.
  3. Wait for confirmation from the school.
  4. Complete the enrollment process.
