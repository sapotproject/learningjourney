About Our School

Learning Journey Child Growth Center, Inc. is committed to supporting every child's growth, learning, and development in a caring school community.

Mission

To provide a caring and supportive learning environment where children can grow, discover, and develop at their own pace.

Vision

To be a school community where learning is meaningful, joyful, and guided by each child's unique journey.

Core Values

  • Care
  • Respect
  • Patience
  • Love for Learning
